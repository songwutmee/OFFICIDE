### Game Concept
OFFICIDE is a doom-like first-person shooter set inside a boring office.

You play as a tired office worker who secretly escapes reality by doodling themselves as a superhero fighting zombies. It is just a small fantasy to survive another dull workday — until zombies suddenly appear for real.

With no proper weapons around, they use what they have. Their finger becomes a finger gun powered by energy built up from endless pointless meetings. Somehow, it actually shoots. For close combat, a simple ballpoint pen turns into a surprisingly deadly weapon that can both sign documents and stab zombies.

The goal is simple: fight through waves of zombies and survive inside the office. The game uses a hand-drawn doodle art style, like sketches in a notebook, to match the imagination theme.

| **Weapon 1** | `1` |
| **Weapon 2** | `2` |
